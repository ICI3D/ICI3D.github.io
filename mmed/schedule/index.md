> The MMED program includes a number of parallel sessions for participants with different backgrounds. <b>Track A</b> is designed for those with a mathematical background, particularly those who have extensive training and/or experience with differential equation models of infectious disease dynamics. <b>Track B</b> is designed for those with a background in epidemiology and/or statistics, particularly those who are involved in data collection for infectious disease systems.

PROGRAM DIRECTORS

Jonathan Dushoff

Program Director for North America

Jonathan is a Professor of Biology in the Department of Biology and Department of Mathematics and Statistics at McMaster University. He has been on the ICI3D Core Faculty since the program started in 2012. He became Program Director for North America in 2018 and is currently Co-Director of the ICI3D Program.

Juliet Pulliam

Program Director for Africa

Juliet is the Director of the South African Centre for Epidemiological Modelling and Analysis (SACEMA) and Professor of Applied Mathematics at Stellenbosch University. She served as Program Director from 2012-2016 and Program Director for Africa from 2016 (to present). She is currently Co-Director of the ICI3D Program.
